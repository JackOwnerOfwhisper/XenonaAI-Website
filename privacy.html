Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how XenonaAI ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our desktop application ("the Application").

Information We Collect

XenonaAI collects the following types of information:

  • API Key: We store your Groq API key locally on your device to facilitate communication with the Groq API.
  • Conversation History: We temporarily store your conversation history within the application to provide context for ongoing conversations.
  • User Preferences: We store your application preferences, such as dark mode settings and ownership mention preferences.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Application
  • Send your requests to the Groq API
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Provide context-aware responses in conversations

Data Storage and Security

All information collected by XenonaAI is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your data on our servers. Your conversations are processed through the Groq API, subject to their privacy policy and terms of service.

Your API key is stored in an environment file (.env) on your local machine. While we implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Third-Party Services

XenonaAI uses the Groq API to process your conversations. When you use our Application, your messages are sent to Groq for processing. Please review Groq's privacy policy to understand how they handle your data.

Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally, you have complete control over it. You can:

  • Clear your conversation history at any time using the clear chat function
  • Delete your API key by removing it from the settings
  • Uninstall the application to remove all associated data

Children's Privacy

XenonaAI is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
